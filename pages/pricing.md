###  Workshop registration fee and what is included

* 300 EUR<br>

<i>The registration fee has been significantly reduced compared to previous editions thanks to sponsorship from the 2AID consortium. The CAMLC organization does not provide additional travel scholarships or funding to cover attendance.</i><br><br>

<u>This fee includes:</u><br>

<p align="justify">
&#9900; Registration to the workshop<br>
&#9900; Three lunches (Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday)<br>
&#9900; Four coffee breaks<br><br>

<u>Cancellation policy:</u><br>
Refunds will not be available, as we need to cover event expenses in advance. If unforeseen circumstances prevent attendance, applicants may transfer their spot to another group member in justified cases.
